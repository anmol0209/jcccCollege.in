Our Facilities

A wide of range of facilities have been provided to the students to ensure a conducive learning atmosphere as well as a pleasant stay in campus.
NSIT ensures that various aspects of student welfare have been well taken care of.

Central Library

Central Library of JCCC COLLEGE is functioning from 1st & Mezzanine Floors of Library Building. Library Building is centrally situated.

Sports

JCCC has a range of facilities including a football ground, cricket ground, basketball court, lawn tennis court, volleyball court, and indoor gymnasium. There is also a running/jogging track on the campus.

Food & Canteens

In addition to the regular mess facilities in the hostels, the JCCC campus boasts of affordable canteens spread throughout the campus. All canteens provide delicious food at reasonable prices.
